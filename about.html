Our story

Thanks for checking us out. We're a husband and wife team that has collected antiques to furnish our own home for a very long time. We love the hunt for "treasure" and to bring run down antiques back to life. In 2008 we opened Sacred Tree Antiques in Notus, Idaho. We needed more space and we found it in Vale, Oregon. After many months of renovation in 2014 we moved to our current location in Vale. We love all the different people we meet and we enjoy sharing our love of antiques. We hope you stop by and find a treasured piece to take to your home.
